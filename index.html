Our Mission

Health is a Right, Not a Privilege.

At Sunrise Health Care Inc., we believe that no one should have to choose between putting food on the table and receiving life-saving medical care. Based in Denver, CO, our mobile clinics and telehealth programs bridge the gap for uninsured and underinsured families.

As a fully registered 501(c)(3) organization, every dollar you give goes directly toward providing tangible medical supplies, mental health counseling, and preventative care.

About Sunrise Health Care Inc.

Transparency, Dedication, and Compassion.

Our Story

Founded in Denver, Colorado, Sunrise Health Care Inc. began as a small group of volunteer nurses and doctors who noticed a terrifying trend: patients returning to the ER repeatedly because they couldn't afford preventative care.

Today, we operate under EIN: 99-0505549 as a fully accredited 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization. We hold ourselves to the highest standards of financial transparency and ethical medical practice.

  • Vision: A world where Zip Code does not determine life expectancy.
  • Mission: To provide free, high-quality medical and psychological care to the vulnerable.
  • Values: Dignity, Equity, Innovation, and Compassion.

Our Programs

Comprehensive care tailored to the specific needs of the Denver community.

Sunrise Mobile Clinics

Our flagship program consists of three retrofitted RVs functioning as fully operational medical clinics. They travel to food banks, homeless shelters, and community centers across Denver.

Impact: Over 5,000 preventative screenings conducted last year, catching 400+ cases of early-stage diabetes and hypertension.

Mental Health Access

We provide up to 12 free sessions of cognitive behavioral therapy to individuals facing severe life stressors, utilizing a network of pro-bono psychologists and licensed clinical social workers.

Impact: 12,000 hours of free therapy provided in the last fiscal year.
