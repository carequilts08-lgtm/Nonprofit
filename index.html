Who We Are

More Than Healthcare — We're a Movement

Sunrise Health Care Inc was born from a simple but powerful belief: every human being deserves access to quality health information, preventive care, and wellness support — regardless of their income, background, or ZIP code.

Based in Denver, Colorado, we've grown from a small volunteer-driven initiative to a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization serving thousands of families. We bring together dedicated healthcare professionals, community organizers, and passionate volunteers united by one mission.

Our programs span preventive health education, mental wellness support, nutrition guidance, chronic disease management, and emergency health assistance — creating a comprehensive safety net for those who need it most.

Our Story

From a Vision to a Movement

Sunrise Health Care Inc was founded in 2018 by a group of healthcare professionals, community organizers, and passionate advocates who witnessed daily the devastating impact of healthcare inequality on Denver's most vulnerable populations. They saw hardworking families forced to choose between medicine and food, seniors going without critical screenings, and children growing up without the health education they deserved.

What started as weekly community health walks and free information sessions in local parks quickly grew into a structured nonprofit organization. By 2019, we had our first official office, a team of 12 dedicated staff members, and partnerships with several local healthcare providers. By 2021, our programs had touched the lives of over 5,000 individuals.

Today, Sunrise Health Care Inc is a thriving 501(c)(3) organization with 48 active programs, 320+ volunteers, and partnerships with leading healthcare institutions, universities, and community organizations. But we haven't forgotten our roots — the belief that every sunrise brings a new opportunity to heal, help, and hope.

Nutrition & Food Access Initiative

Food insecurity and poor nutrition are root causes of many preventable diseases. Our Nutrition & Food Access Initiative addresses this head-on through monthly healthy food distributions, weekly nutrition counseling sessions, and practical cooking classes that teach families how to prepare nutritious, affordable meals.

Mobile Health Clinics

Our two fully-equipped mobile health units are lifelines for communities without easy access to healthcare facilities. Last Updated: January 1, 2025 | Sunrise Health Care Inc | EIN: 99-0505549 | 501(c)(3) Tax-Exempt Organization

Sunrise Health Care Inc is deeply grateful for the generosity of our donors. This policy outlines how we handle charitable donations, recurring giving arrangements, and requests for refunds or cancellations. We are committed to being transparent and fair in all our financial dealings.

1. Nature of Donations

All monetary contributions made to Sunrise Health Care Inc are charitable donations to a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit organization. Donations are not payments for goods or services and are generally non-refundable unless made in error. All donations, whether one-time or recurring, will receive a written acknowledgment for tax purposes within 48 hours of processing.

2. Tax Deductibility

Sunrise Health Care Inc is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization (EIN: 99-0505549). Donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. We provide tax receipts for all donations, including the organization's name, EIN, donation amount, date, and a statement that no goods or services were provided in exchange (unless applicable). Please consult your tax advisor regarding the deductibility of your specific donation.

3. Recurring Donations

When you establish a recurring (monthly or annual) donation, you authorize Sunrise Health Care Inc to charge your payment method on a regular schedule until you cancel. You may cancel your recurring donation at any time by:

  • Emailing info@sunrisehealthcareinc.org with "Cancel Recurring Donation" in the subject line
  • Contacting us by phone at (720) 555-0198 during business hours
  • Writing to 6784 E Cedar Ave, Apt 607, Denver, CO 80224

Cancellation requests must be received at least 5 business days before the next scheduled charge to avoid that payment. Charges already processed cannot be reversed unless they meet our refund criteria below.

4. Refund Policy

While donations are generally considered final and non-refundable, we recognize that circumstances sometimes warrant a refund. We will consider refund requests in the following situations:

  • Duplicate Donations: If a technical error caused you to be charged more than once for the same intended donation
  • Unauthorized Transaction: If a donation was made fraudulently without your authorization
  • Data Entry Error: If you accidentally donated a significantly different amount than intended (e.g., $500 instead of $50)
  • Technical Processing Error: If a technical failure caused an erroneous charge

To request a refund, please email info@sunrisehealthcareinc.org with the following information: your full name, email address used for the donation, donation date and amount, transaction ID (from your receipt), and reason for the refund request. We will respond within 5 business days with our determination. Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 10 business days.

Donations designated for specific programs or campaigns will be used for those purposes. If a designated program is discontinued or the designated amount exceeds the program's needs, we will contact the donor to discuss redirecting the gift with their approval.

Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making our website and services accessible to everyone

Last Updated: January 1, 2025 | Sunrise Health Care Inc | EIN: 99-0505549

Sunrise Health Care Inc is committed to ensuring that our website and digital content are accessible to all people, including those with disabilities. We believe that everyone deserves equal access to health information and resources, and this commitment extends to our digital presence.

1. Our Commitment

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities, including those who are blind or have low vision, deaf or hard of hearing, have cognitive or learning disabilities, or have motor impairments.

2. Accessibility Features of Our Website

We have implemented the following accessibility features on our website:

  • Text Alternatives: All images include descriptive alt text for screen readers
  • Keyboard Navigation: Our website is fully navigable using keyboard controls
  • Color Contrast: We maintain sufficient color contrast ratios throughout the site for readability
  • Readable Fonts: We use clear, readable fonts at appropriate sizes, with no text rendered in images
  • Scalable Text: Content is designed to remain usable when text is scaled up to 200%
  • Form Labels: All form fields include descriptive labels associated with their inputs
  • Link Descriptions: Links are written to be descriptive and meaningful out of context
  • Heading Structure: We use proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3) for logical page structure
  • Language Declaration: Page language is declared in the HTML for screen readers
  • Focus Indicators: Keyboard focus indicators are visible throughout the site
  • No Flashing Content: We avoid content that flashes at rates known to cause seizures

3. Known Limitations

We are continuously working to improve accessibility. Some areas that are works-in-progress include: certain older PDF documents in our resource library may not be fully accessible; embedded third-party content (maps, social media widgets) may not meet all accessibility standards; and some complex data visualizations may not have full text alternatives. We are actively addressing these issues.

4. Assistive Technologies Supported

Our website has been tested with and is designed to work with the following assistive technologies: NVDA Screen Reader (Windows), VoiceOver (macOS and iOS), JAWS Screen Reader (Windows), and TalkBack (Android). We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best accessible experience.

5. Accessibility in Our Programs

Beyond our website, Sunrise Health Care Inc is committed to physical accessibility in all our programs and activities:

  • All our program venues are selected for wheelchair accessibility
  • We provide materials in large print upon request
  • Interpreter services are available for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community
  • Health materials are available in plain language at appropriate reading levels
  • We provide materials in multiple languages, including Spanish
  • Our mobile clinic units are equipped with accessibility ramps

6. Feedback and Contact

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our website and programs. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, need assistance accessing any content, or would like to request an accommodation, please contact us:

  • Email: info@sunrisehealthcareinc.org (with "Accessibility" in the subject line)
  • Mail: Sunrise Health Care Inc, 6784 E Cedar Ave, Apt 607, Denver, CO 80224
  • Response Time: We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days

7. Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response to an accessibility concern, you may contact the Colorado Civil Rights Division or file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Justice regarding website accessibility under Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

8. Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We conduct regular accessibility audits of our website, train our staff and content creators on accessibility best practices, incorporate accessibility requirements into our content creation and web development processes, and monitor and respond to user feedback on accessibility issues. This Accessibility Statement will be reviewed and updated at least annually.
