The Silent Crisis

Bridging the Gap in a Fractured System

In our modern society, thousands of individuals wake up every day having to make an impossible choice: pay for groceries, keep the electricity on, or afford life-saving medication. The cost of healthcare has skyrocketed, leaving marginalized communities, hourly workers, and undocumented families out in the cold.

Sunrise Health Care Inc. was born out of necessity. We recognized that emergency rooms were filling up with patients suffering from preventable conditions—conditions that escalated simply because they couldn't afford a primary care checkup.

We take medicine directly to the streets. We don't wait for the vulnerable to find us; we mobilize our resources to meet them where they live, work, and pray. Our approach is holistic, treating not just physical ailments but the psychological trauma that accompanies poverty and marginalization.

  • Complete removal of financial barriers to treatment.
  • Culturally competent care with multi-lingual staff.
  • Direct intervention before conditions become critical.
How We Help

Our Four Pillars of Community Care

We do not believe in band-aid solutions. Our comprehensive programs are designed to uplift entire communities through sustained, multi-disciplinary healthcare support.

Mobile Clinics

Fully equipped retrofitted RVs that travel to food banks, community centers, and shelters, providing on-the-spot physicals, diagnostics, and essential medications.

Pediatric Wellness

Ensuring the next generation grows up healthy with free immunizations, nutritional counseling, vision screenings, and pediatric dental care.

Mental Health

Eradicating the stigma of mental illness by offering free, private counseling sessions for trauma, depression, addiction recovery, and family counseling.

Prescription Relief

A dedicated pharmacy fund that covers the cost of chronic disease medications like insulin, asthma inhalers, and blood pressure control drugs.

Our Story

A Vision Born from Empathy

Founded in Denver, Colorado, Sunrise Health Care Inc. began as a small group of volunteer nurses and doctors who noticed a terrifying trend: patients returning to the ER repeatedly because they couldn't afford basic preventative care.

Today, we operate under EIN: 99-0505549 as a fully accredited 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization. We hold ourselves to the highest standards of financial transparency and ethical medical practice.

  • Vision: A world where Zip Code does not determine life expectancy.
  • Mission: To provide free, high-quality medical and psychological care to the vulnerable.
  • Values: Dignity, Equity, Innovation, and Compassion.

Our Programs

Comprehensive care tailored to community needs.

Flagship Initiative

Sunrise Mobile Clinics

Our flagship program consists of three retrofitted RVs functioning as fully operational medical clinics. They travel to food banks, homeless shelters, and community centers across Denver.

Impact: Over 5,000 preventative screenings conducted last year, catching 400+ cases of early-stage diabetes and hypertension.

Mental Wellness

Mental Health Access

We provide up to 12 free sessions of cognitive behavioral therapy to individuals facing severe life stressors, utilizing a network of pro-bono psychologists and licensed clinical social workers.

Impact: 12,000 hours of free therapy provided in the last fiscal year, dramatically lowering local crisis intervention rates.
